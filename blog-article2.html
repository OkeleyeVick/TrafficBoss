How Do I Start Receiving Views

To start receiving views on your links would have to:
  • Activate any of the membership
  • Create a link
  • Start Console
  • Create a link
    • Click on the Dashboard on the Home Page
    • On the left and corner of the sidebar, click on the tab
    • Then select add a link
      • In put the name of the link. This name should be an identifier of the link
      • Enter the URL of the link i.e TraffikBoss.com
      • Then you enter the parent of the link if you want to create a hierarchy between links (this would help identify links that belong to same parents, and also allow the system to prioritize visits) or leave as none at this point
      • Select the link type we have some categories, so select from the list
      • Here you can set the percentage of the weight of your overall traffic that would be given to this link
  • Start a console
    • Click on Dashboard on the Homepage
    • On the left and corner of the sidebar, click on the console tab
    • Then click on the start button. Now you have successfully started a console, just wait and watch the links. (In case you might h have difficulties watching the link, you might need to disable popups for your browsers)
  • Activate any of the Membership, either Basic, Premium, or Affiliate
    • On the home page, click on signup
      • Enter your username
      • Enter your Email
      • Enter your Password
      • Then Retype the password
      • Agree to the terms and Register
      • Note, to activate your email
    • Activate a Membership
      • On signup, you would be directed to a Membership page
      • Select the membership of your choice.
